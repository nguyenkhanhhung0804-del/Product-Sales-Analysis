# Product Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow using **SQL** and **Power BI** to analyze product sales performance. Multiple datasets were integrated to uncover business insights related to revenue, profit, discounts, and product performance. The SQL analysis was used to answer key business questions, while Power BI was used to build an interactive dashboard for data visualization.

## Objectives

* Analyze sales and profitability across products.
* Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
* Measure the impact of discounts on sales and profit.
* Create an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

## Dataset

The project uses the following datasets:

* `Product_data.csv`
* `product_sales.csv`
* `discount_data.csv`

## Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** – Data cleaning, joins, aggregations, CTEs, window functions, and business analysis.
* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard development and KPI visualization.
* **CSV** – Source data.

## Key Business Questions

* What are the top 10 most profitable products?
* Which products generate high revenue but low profit?
* How do discounts affect revenue and profitability?
* Which products contribute the most to total sales?
* What are the monthly and yearly sales trends?
